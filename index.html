The structure

90 days. Three phases. One outcome.

01

Diagnose

We identify the exact decision you are avoiding and remove all ambiguity around it. We examine the pattern that has allowed the delay to continue and make the cost of staying exactly where you are explicit.

You leave this phase with clarity that is practical, not philosophical.

02

Decide

Once the decision is defined, we eliminate half-measures. We establish the standard you are choosing to live by and create a clear execution plan with specific milestones and deadlines.

The decision is no longer something you are considering. It is something you are preparing to execute.

03

Execute

You take the action. You do not retreat into overthinking when discomfort arises. You do not renegotiate your commitment. We then stabilise the aftermath to ensure you do not backpedal once the action has been taken.

By the end of 90 days, you will have followed through.
