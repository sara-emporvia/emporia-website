Common questions

Everything you need
to know.

Is this therapy?

No. This is structured coaching focused on decision-making and execution. If you're looking for trauma processing or clinical support, therapy is the right space. If you're functional but stuck between knowing and doing, this is built for that.

What if I already know what I need to do?

You probably do. This work isn't about giving you advice. It's about identifying the subconscious resistance preventing you from acting on what you already know.

What if I'm not sure which decision to focus on?

You don't need to be clear to get started. What seems like confusion is often fear of commitment. Our session will help you tell the difference.

How is this different from other coaching?

Most coaching focuses on insight and awareness. This focuses on execution. I don't placate people or validate excuses. I hold up a mirror and ask the questions that surface your own answers.

What if I fall back into old patterns?

That means we haven't fully addressed the deeper beliefs about who you are. Sustainable execution comes from shifting how you see yourself, not just what you plan to do.

Is coaching confidential?

Absolutely. Everything shared during coaching is kept completely private and confidential.
