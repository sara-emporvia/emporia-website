Common questions

Everything you need
to know.

Is this therapy?

No. This is structured coaching focused on decision-making and execution. If you're looking for trauma processing or clinical mental health support, therapy is the right space. If you're high-functioning but stuck between knowing and doing, this is built for that.

What if I already know what I need to do?

You probably do. This work isn't about giving you advice or more information. It's about identifying the subconscious resistance preventing you from acting on what you already know.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Payment plans are available for both containers and can be discussed during your clarity call. The single session is paid in full at booking.

What if I'm not sure which option is right for me?

Book a free clarity call. We'll identify exactly where you are and which container if any is the right fit. Not everyone is accepted and that conversation goes both ways.

How is this different from other coaching?

Most coaching focuses on insight and awareness. This focuses on execution. I work with what you don't realise you're saying the language patterns, the safety mechanisms, the specific triggers that cause retreat. Everything you receive is made for your situation, not a template.

Is coaching confidential?

Completely. Everything shared during sessions is kept private and confidential. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.

What if I fall back into old patterns after a session?

That's what the tools are for. The pattern interrupt audio, the sabotage map, the retreat protocol these exist precisely for those moments. And if it's happening consistently, it means there's a deeper layer to address, which is what the containers are designed for.

What is the cancellation policy?

24 hours' notice is required to reschedule any session. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be forfeited. See Terms & Conditions for full details.
