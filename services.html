Common questions

Everything you need
to know.

What if I already know what I need to do?

You probably do. This work isn't about giving you advice or more information. It's about identifying the subconscious resistance preventing you from acting on what you already know. Most high-functioning adults don't lack clarity — they lack alignment between identity and action.

How is this different from therapy?

Therapy helps you work through past experiences and improve mental health. Coaching looks forward and focuses on specific outcomes. We start with the result you want, identify what's in the way, and build a clear path to get there. If you need clinical mental health support, therapy is the right fit. If you're ready to make a real change and follow through on it, this is built for that.

What if I'm just overthinking and need to push through?

Overthinking usually isn't about willpower. Most of the time it's your mind's way of keeping you safe. If simply pushing through was enough, you'd have done it by now. The work here identifies what's actually causing the hesitation so you can move past it properly.

What if I'm not 100% sure what decision I need to make?

You don't have to be completely clear to get started. What seems like confusion is often fear of commitment. The session will help you tell the difference between fear and genuine uncertainty, so you can move with confidence either way.

Is this only for career-related decisions?

No. This work applies to any significant decision you've been avoiding — career changes, relationships, moving, creative risks, leadership decisions, or any major life choice that keeps getting pushed to "later."

What if I fall back into old patterns after a session?

That's what the tools are for. The pattern interrupt audio, the sabotage map, the retreat protocol — these exist precisely for those moments. If it's happening consistently, it means there's a deeper layer to address, which is what the containers are designed for.

How do I know if I'm a good fit?

You'll likely be a good fit if you're self-aware, capable in most areas of your life, and frustrated by your own delay. This isn't for people who want motivation or hype. It's for people who are ready to act. Book a free clarity call and we'll know within the first few minutes.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Payment plans are available for both containers and can be discussed during your clarity call. The single session is paid in full at booking.
